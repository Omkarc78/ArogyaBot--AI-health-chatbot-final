Welcome to Our AI Health Platform

AI-powered image analysis helps detect potential vitamin deficiencies by examining visual signs on the skin, eyes, hair, and nails. Subtle changes such as dryness, discoloration, or texture irregularities can indicate deficiencies in vitamins like A, B12, C, or D. This technology uses machine learning to analyze images and identify patterns that may not be easily visible to the human eye, offering a fast, non-invasive, and accessible tool for early detection and personalized health insights.
